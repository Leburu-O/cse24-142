GTA 6 DLC is already disappointing fans, and we get it

GTA VI will be a ridiculously big game, but since it will probably be over a decade until GTA VII is even close to launch, Rockstar's next game needs DLC, single player DLC specifically. We all know why it is so that players are forced to jump into GTA V: Online, but for those of us who do not enjoy getting grieved by other players or endlessly grinding heists for pocket change, it’s not exactly the best experience.
