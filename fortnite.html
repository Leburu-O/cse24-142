Fortnite

Platform: All Platforms | Genre: Battle Royale

Fortnite Battle Royale is a player-versus-player game for up to 100 players, allowing one to play alone, in a duo, or in a squad (usually consisting of three or four players).

In Fortnite, players drop onto an ever-shrinking map to battle enemies while scavenging weapons, building structures, and surviving as long as possible. With regular updates, new seasons, and limited-time events, Fortnite remains one of the most popular online games across PC, console, and mobile devices.
